Our Story

From Tree to Table

Every MapleMoon product begins with Australian-grown carob pods, sun-ripened and hand-harvested from century-old trees across South Australia. We mill, roast, and craft each bar, moon, and elixir in small batches, honouring the slow rhythms of the land.

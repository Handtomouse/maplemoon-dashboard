From Tree to Table

Every MapleMoon product begins with Australian-grown carob pods, sun-ripened and hand-harvested from century-old trees across South Australia. We mill, roast, and craft each bar, moon, and elixir in small batches, honouring the slow rhythms of the land. No cacao, no caffeine, no compromise — just pure, wholefood carob the way nature intended it.
